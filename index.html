Behind the Lens

About Me

I am a Photography and Multimedia Technician in my 3rd year of studies, developing an eye for anything worth capturing — if it's interesting, it's worth a shot.

With over a 5 years behind the lens, I've shot across multiple countries, from the mountains of Italy to the most iconic and beautiful streets of Vienna or Barcelona. Every image in this portfolio is a fragment of a larger story waiting to be told.

My work spans landscape, portrait, composition, and much more. I believe the best photograph is the one that stops your breath — not because it's perfect, but because it's true.

How I Work

The Process

01

Vision & Concept

Every shoot begins with an idea — a feeling, a colour palette, a story. I spend as much time thinking as I do shooting, because intention shapes everything.

02

The Shoot

I work slowly and deliberately, often returning to a location at different hours to find the exact quality of light I'm after. Patience is the secret ingredient.

03

The Editing Process

Post-processing is where the mood is sealed. I develop each image the way a painter layers glaze — building depth, pulling shadows, finding the image within the image.
